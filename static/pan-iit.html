Welcome to Pan-IIT Odia Association

This is the association of Odia students and faculty members / staffs of all the Indian Institute of Technology ( IITs). Currently the constituent associations are Utkala Association IITM, Utkala Parivar IITK, Kalinga Samaj IITKGP, Utkala Parishad IITB, Odia Parivar IITD, and Utkala Samaj IITG. The objective of the association is to organize various activities & functions to celebrate the culture and tradition of ODISHA. Also to take meaningful initiatives to build a prosperous ODISHA.
